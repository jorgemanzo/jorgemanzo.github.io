A Note About Negative Numbers

This app will convert your negative input into raw binary. Then it will perform two's complement on your
binary. Then this complemented binary is written as output. Another note, if your negative input is Hexadecimal,
for example, then in the Hexadecimal output we just copy and paste your input into output.


A Note About Output Binary from Negative Numbers.

Remember, this app converts your input into binary, and then performs two's complement on that binary.
Unless you give us negative binary, we will be writing as output the two's complement of your Binary.
