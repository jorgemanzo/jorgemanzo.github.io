A note about negative numbers

Because of how this app handles negative numbers, you need to ignore the output that matches the type of your input.
For example, if you input a negative Decimal number, ignore the Decimal number in the Output as it will not be correct.
If your input is a negative Hexadecimal, ignore the Hex output.

This app will convert your input into binary, append two leading zeros, and then perform two's complement.
